Discover Authentic Khmer Cuisine

Want a unique activity during your Siem Reap visit?

A Siem Reap cooking class is the perfect break from exploring temples.

Get ready for a memorable culinary experience in Siem Reap! Your journey starts with a guided market tour, where Chef Ly Seng will introduce you to the fresh ingredients essential to Cambodian cooking.

Back at our restaurant, you'll have your own personal cooking station. Your chef will guide you step by step as you prepare classic Khmer dishes like spring rolls, Lotus salad with pork belly, Cabbage salad with chicken, Fried Lok lak with mushrooms, or the famous amok fish.

After your hands-on session, the class ends with everyone enjoying the incredible meal they've created together.

We provide complimentary tuk-tuk transport to and from your hotel.

The experience is 2-3 hours long, depending on whether you include the market visit.
